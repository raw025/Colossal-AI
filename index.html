Hi, I am a first-year Ph.D student at Texas A&M University, supervised by Prof. Zhangyang Wang. I received my undergraduate degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China. My interests include Deep Learning and Computer Vision. Now I work on the area of generative model and image enhancement.

I work at Bytedance AI Lab as a research intern supervised by Dr. Jianchao Yang, Dr. Ding Liu, Dr. Chen Fang, and Dr. Xiaohui Shen from 2019 spring. During my undergraduate year, I also work as a Research Assistant with Prof. Pan Zhou (HUST) and Dr. Yu Cheng (Microsoft AI Research).
